ARIMA(1,1,1) Formula Used

Step 1: Difference = Current Price - Previous Price

Step 2: Forecast Difference = (φ₁ × Last Difference) + (θ₁ × Last Error) + Random Noise

Step 3: Predicted Price = Last Price + Forecast Difference

Notes: φ₁ = AR factor, θ₁ = MA factor, Random Noise = small random value
